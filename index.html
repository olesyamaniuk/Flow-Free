Connect. Flow. Solve!

game description

  • Flow Free: Shapes takes the classic Flow Free gameplay to the next level by introducing new shapes, intricate paths, and limitless possibilities. Connect matching colors with pipes to create a Flow®, ensuring that all colors are paired and the entire board is covered. But be careful—pipes will break if they overlap or cross!
  • With hundreds of puzzles to solve, various difficulty levels, and relaxing yet challenging gameplay, Flow Free: Shapes offers an engaging puzzle experience for players of all ages. Whether you're looking for a casual brain teaser or a mind-bending challenge, this game is the perfect way to keep your brain sharp while having fun!
